CONSIOUS HARVEST

We aim to combat the degradation of soil by giving appropriate crop recommendation.

THE GREAT MAJORITY OF INDIAN FARMERS RELY ON THEIR INTUITION TO DETERMINE WHICH CROP TO PLANT IN A GIVEN SEASON. THEY TAKE COMFORT IN SIMPLY FOLLOWING ANCESTRAL FARMING PRACTISES AND STANDARDS, OBLIVIOUS TO THE FACT THAT CROP YIELD IS HIGHLY DEPENDENT ON CURRENT WEATHER AND SOIL CONDITIONS.THIS SYSTEM SUGGESTS CROP BASED ON SOIL CLASSIFICATION WITH ASSEMBLING CLASSIFIERS SYSTEM HAS BEEN DEVELOPED AFTER EXAMINING SEVERAL PREVIOUS SYSTEMS ON CROP PREDICTION.
